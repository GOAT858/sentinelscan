About the Inventor

Sameer Mana

Student Researcher | 2026 3M Young Scientist Challenge

Project Sentinel started from one question: what if we could detect pancreatic risk signals before symptoms appear, using tools that are affordable and simple to deploy?

Base

California, United States

Focus

Translating high-dimensional biomarker research into a practical, low-cost strip workflow.

Why This Project

Pancreatic cancer outcomes are often poor because detection is too late. Sentinel is designed to make early signal screening more reachable, with a camera-driven process that supports faster decisions and follow-up.
